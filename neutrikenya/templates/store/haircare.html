Hair Care Guide

Cleansing

Wash your hair 2-3 times a week with our gentle, sulfate-free shampoos to remove buildup without stripping natural oils.

Conditioning

Always follow with a conditioner, focusing on the ends. Once a week, treat your hair with our deep conditioning mask.

Protection

Before heat styling, apply our leave-in treatment or growth spray to protect hair from damage and promote growth.
